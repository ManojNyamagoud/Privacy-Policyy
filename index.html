Privacy Policy for FocusLock

Last updated: March 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

FocusLock ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. NO DATA COLLECTION

FocusLock does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data remains exclusively on your device.

3. PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED

FocusLock requires certain permissions to function:

  • Accessibility Service: Used solely to detect which app is currently in the foreground. This allows us to track your usage time and enforce your self-set limits. We do not read, store, or transmit any content from other apps.
  • Usage Statistics: Used to track how much time you spend on different apps. This data stays on your device and is never shared.
  • Notifications: Used to alert you when you're approaching your daily limits.

4. DATA STORAGE

All your data - including app limits, usage history, and focus sessions - is stored locally in an encrypted database on your device. No cloud storage or servers are used.

5. OFFLINE FUNCTIONALITY

FocusLock works completely offline. No internet connection is required, and no data is ever sent to the internet.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

7. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app.

8. CONTACT US

9. OPEN SOURCE

FocusLock is built with privacy as the primary concern. All code is designed to ensure your data stays on your device.
